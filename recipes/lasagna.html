Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish originated from Italy.

It has layers of lasagna noodles that contains filling and sauces in each layers. Making lasagna may be time-consuming But its worth it wait.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pount lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1(28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2(6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2(6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chooped fresh parsely, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sal, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon gorund black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces rocotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
