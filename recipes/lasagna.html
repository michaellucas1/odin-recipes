Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish originated from Italy.

It has layers of lasagna noodles that contains filling and sauces in each layers.

Making lasagna can be time-consuming.But its worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet italian sausage
  • 3/4 pount lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1(28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2(6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2(6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chooped fresh parsely, divided
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons sal, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon gorund black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces rocotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all your ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    Cook sausage, ground beef, and garlic in a Dutch over over medium heat until well browned.

  3. Step 3

    Stire in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Seacon with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hourse, stirring occasionally.

  4. Step 4

    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

  5. Step 5

    In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

  6. Step 6

    Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).

  7. Step 7

    To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.

  8. Step 8

    Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

  9. Step 9

    Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foild and bake for an additional 25 minutes.

  10. Step 10

    Rest lasanagna for 15 minutes before serving.
